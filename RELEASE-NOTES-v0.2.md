## The v0.2 Idea

> **A Milestone is the smallest governable unit of verifiable product value toward
> the Product Outcome — not a unit of technical execution.**

No new protocol. No new lifecycle state. The Implementation Plan becomes a
better contract.

---

## What Changes

### Milestone redefinition (GS-14 / GR-14)

Each milestone MUST declare: **Product Value**, **Consumer**, **Observable Result**,
**Evidence**, **Implementation Tasks**.

### GS-13 / GR-13

Removed the infrastructure milestone exception.

### Milestone Audit

Primary question: **does the promised product value exist?**
