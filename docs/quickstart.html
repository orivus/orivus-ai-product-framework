Create your first
AI-native product

A CTO does not need every specification on day one. You need a governed path from opportunity to verified outcome.

The path

Four steps. One Intention. Evidence at every gate.

1

Product Discovery

A real need appears. Before building, remove uncertainty: who needs this, what must be true, what is still unknown.

Framework: Discovery lifecycle → Product Foundation readiness

2

Product Foundation

Enough governed knowledge exists to build safely: scope boundaries, constraints, and the product's governing artifacts.

Framework: Product Foundation readiness

3

First Intention

Scope the smallest governed unit of change. One measurable outcome. One Implementation Plan with milestones executed one at a time.

Framework: Intention → Milestones → VERIFY → AUDIT → PASS

4

Verified Product Outcome

The capability exists and is proven by evidence — not by model confidence. A human reviews. Knowledge is synchronized. The Intention closes.

Framework: Product Outcome Realization + Human Review + Product Knowledge Synchronization (PKS)

✓

Institutional Knowledge

The product is easier to evolve. The next Intention starts from stronger Product Knowledge — not from a blank chat session.
